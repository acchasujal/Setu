SCHOLARSHIP NOTICE – ACADEMIC YEAR 2024–25

This is to inform all parents and students that the Government Merit Scholarship for the academic year 2024–25 is now open.

Students studying in Class 8 to Class 12 whose family annual income is below ₹2,50,000 are eligible to apply for this scholarship.

Required Documents:

Income Certificate (issued by Tahsildar)

Caste Certificate (if applicable)

Student Aadhaar Card

Bank Passbook copy

School Bonafide Certificate

Important Dates:

Last date to submit the application: 20 July 2024

Applications submitted after the last date will not be accepted.

Submission Process:

Parents must submit all required documents to the school office for verification. After verification, the school will upload the application on the government portal.

Important Note:

If documents are incomplete or the application is submitted after the deadline, the student will not receive the scholarship benefit.

For any queries, please contact the school office during working hours.

— School Administration
